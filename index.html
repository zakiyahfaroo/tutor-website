Reading Story 📖

The Little Star

Once upon a time, there was a little star who lived high up in the night sky. Every night, she would shine as brightly as she could to help travelers find their way home. One stormy night, dark clouds covered the sky and she could not shine through. She felt sad and worried. But she remembered that even when clouds hide the light, the light is still there. The next morning, when the clouds cleared, she shone brighter than ever before.

Answer These Questions:

1. What did the little star do every night?

2. How did the star feel during the storm? Why?

3. What lesson did the star learn?

Grammar Rules Made Easy! ✨

📌 Nouns

A noun is a person, place, thing, or idea.

👤 dog 🏫 school 🍎 apple 💭 happiness
🏃 Verbs

A verb is an action or state of being.

🏃 run 😴 sleep 🎨 paint 💭 think
🎨 Adjectives

An adjective describes a noun.

🌈 colorful 🐘 big 🌸 beautiful ⚡ fast

Practice: Find the nouns, verbs, and adjectives!

Shapes and Geometry! 🔷

⬜

Square

4 equal sides, 4 corners

Area = side × side

▭

Rectangle

2 pairs of equal sides

Area = length × width

🔺

Triangle

3 sides, 3 corners

Area = ½ × base × height

⭕

Circle

No corners, perfectly round

Area = π × radius²

Can you solve this?

A rectangle is 5 units long and 3 units wide. What is its area?
